The Best Movies According to Maha

My top 3 films of all-time!

The Shape of Water

This is my very favorite film. Extremely artistic and beautifully shot with amazing characters.

The Beauty and The Beast

A classic. Beautiful 2-D animation on top of catchy songs and a strange love story.

Devdas

A bollywood classic. A tradegy from start to finish with a beautiful range of colors and amazing cinematography.
